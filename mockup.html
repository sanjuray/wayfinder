About wayfinder

Wayfinder is for everyone in between.

For the enthusiastic and the lazy. For ambitious visionary planners and the hard-to-move homebodies with glimpses of hope. For the seasoned wanderer and the once-in-a-while explorer. For anyone collecting places they mean to go.

Wayfinder doesn't push. It pins. It plans. It taunts when you need it, encourages when you don't, and stays quiet when you just want to look at the map.

Built for fellow travelers — hoping this helps in your journey.

Made with care. No servers. No ads. No telemetry. Version 1.0.0 · 2026.

Map provider

Most users won't need to change this. Wayfinder defaults to OpenStreetMap tiles. Pick a different one if you want a different look.

Behavior

Fine-grained control over what wayfinder does on its own.
Taunt mode
Allow the sharper quotes (the ones that roast you for not going).
Dynamic quote conditions
Quotes that reference your specific saved/visited counts.
Auto-shift to dusk after sunset
Map switches to a darker theme automatically at night.

Sidebar order: main settings → About → Advanced (italicized, separated). Click "Advanced" to reveal Map provider + Behavior controls.

your data isn't backed up

You've added 3 places and edited a collection since your last export. Close the tab and they're safe in browser storage — but if you ever clear it, they're gone. Want to save a backup first?
